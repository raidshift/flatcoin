FlatCoin (FLAT) on TRON Blockchain !

  • FlatCoin (FLAT) is an ERC20 compliant Meme Token powered by the TRON Blockchain.
  • The total supply of FLAT is limited to 1 000 000 tokens of which the initial sole owner is a Smart Contract.
  • As no other party has access to any tokens owned by the Smart Contract the only way to initially obtain FLAT is to conduct a FLAT  USDT - Token Swap. Insofar the Smart Contract operates as an Automated Market Maker (AMM) by analogy with Decentralized Exchanges (DEX).
  • A Token Swap can be conducted by either withdrawing FLAT from or depositing FLAT to the Smart Contract. Please refer to White Paper for more information on token mechanics.
  • To swap FLAT  USDT and read price data from the Smart Contract please connect your TronLink Wallet (available on PC / iOS / Android).
