FLAT ERIC is back !

Featuring FlatCoin (FLAT) on TRON Blockchain

  • FlatCoin (FLAT) is an ERC20 compliant Meme Token on TRON Blockchain
  • The total supply of FLAT is limited to 1 000 000 Tokens which were entirely locked in an immutable Smart Contract at launch
  • FLAT can be acquired from the Contract in decentralized exchange (DEX) for USDT and vice versa via a feeless Token Swap
  • Current FLAT price is determined by the amount of locked FLAT and USDT in the Contract (Refer to White Paper for exact mechanics)
  • Initial price for FLAT was 0.001 USDT at launch and can programatically never be lower
  • To interact with the Contract connect TronLink to your TRON account (available on PC / iOS / Android)
